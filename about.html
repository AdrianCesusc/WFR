Why are we doing this?

Thousands of refugees face a disproportionate lack of opportunities in the labour market. Recognizing this, we are building a page for refugees to have equal access to employment opportunities.

Our goal:

Support refugees and asylum seekers seeking to start life again here in Brazil, through the insertion and integration of them in job opportunities in Brazil.
